Simple Omelette recipe

An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal.This omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection,optionally filled with your choice of cheese,vegtables, or meats.

Preparation Time
  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Prepartion: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

    • Ingredients

    • 2-3 large eggs
    • Salt,to taste
    • Pepper,to taste
    • One table spoon butter (or)Oil
    • Optional fillings:cheese,diced vegtables,cooked meats,herbs

    Instructions

    1. Beat the Eggs: In a bowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed.You can add a tablespoon of water of milk for a fluffier texture.
    2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
    3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling,pour the eggs .Tilt the pan ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
    4. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook,carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings.Let it cook for another minute,then slide ir onto a plate.
    5. Enjoy: Serve hot,with additional salt and pepper if needed.
